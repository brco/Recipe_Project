Chicken Enchiladas!

A quick and easy take on a classic Mexican favourite!

Ingredients

Click ingredients to cross them off your shopping list!
  • 1lb chicken
  • 1 package taco seasoning
  • 1 cup water
  • 6 to 8 large tortilla shells
  • 3 cups cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups enchilada sauce
  • sour cream
  • green onion
  • salsa

Optional Ingredients

  • diced jalapeno peppers
  • sliced black olives

Directions

Click here to hide all images
  1. Heat a skillet on medium with about a tablespoon of oil. Cook chicken about 7 minutes on each side or until no longer pink.
  2. Once cooked, tranfer your chicken to a cutting board and shred it with a fork. Add your chicken back to the skillet, add taco seasoning and 1 cup of water and let simmer until thickened.
  3. Lay out the tortillas and evenly distribute the cooked chicken. Add a small amount of cheese to your enchilada filling. Add any of the optional fillings you may have chosen to use.
  4. Pour a small amount of enchilada sauce into your baking dish to coat the bottom. Begin rolling the enchiladas and placing them into your baking dish.
  5. Once all enchiladas are placed in the dish, top them with the remaining enchilada sauce and cheese.
  6. Bake the enchiladas in a preheated 325 degree oven for 30 minutes or until the cheese is thoroughly melted
  7. Top your enchiladas with sour cream, salsa and green onions. Serve with some mexican rice, refried beans or a side salad.
